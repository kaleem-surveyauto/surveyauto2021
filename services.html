Retail Solutions

Retail audit has traditionally been used to estimate and track market shares both in terms of value, volume and distribution (numeric and weighted). However traditional syndicated retail audit tracking studies have its own set of challenges, more specifically capturing data for more than 60 categories depending mostly on shop owners’ memory. Over the years, retail audit has hence become inaccurate, time consuming and fairly expensive.

SurveyAuto propose an alternative approach which start with your secondary sales and retail landscape data and built customized panels which are more relevant to your brands and competition as well.

Population & SEC Maps

SurveyAuto started with a mission to resolve fundamental population of developing world i.e. lack of accurate population data. Non-availability of accurate population estimates was one of the key barriers to data driven policy making. With this objective, we have been able to estimate population based on SATELLITE IMAGERY analysis.

SurveyAuto platform identifies built-up areas, houses and any other structures/ establishments and utilizes latest advancement in AI and machine learning to estimate population at a 100m2 granularity. In addition, we also classify localities based on their socio-economic outlook (SEC A,B,C,D and E). Our platform utilizes numerous parameters to evaluate socio-economic classification including but not restricted to night light imagery, size of houses, property and retail rates, gridized vs. non-gridized communities, size and quality of roads and more.

Our clients has utilized this data for opportunity sizing and geography prioritization for business expansion.
